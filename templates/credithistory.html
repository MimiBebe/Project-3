Loan Statistics - Credit History

Of all the loan applicants in our model, 78% had a good credit history (1), while 23% had either a bad one or no credit history (0).
As you can see from the loan approval chart, loan applicants who had a good credit history were approved at a significatnly higher rate.
We can assume this is one of the most important factors in the loan approval decision from lenders.
