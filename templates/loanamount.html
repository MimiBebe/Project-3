Loan Statistics - Amount Requested

Of all the loan applicants in our model:

  • 60% requested a loan amount between $100-$200k
  • 25.9% requested a loan amount of less than $100k
  • 11.4% requested a loan amount between $200k-$400k
  • 2.3% requested a loan amount between $400k-$800k
As you can see from the loan approval chart, the loan approval % was fairly even across all loan amounts with the exception of $200k-$400k.
