Random Forests

Income (green in bar graphs), whether as Total Income, Applicant Income alone, or log(Total Income), was the highest-ranked factor in all Random Forests tested. Loan Amount (blue) and Credit Ranking (red) were the other high-ranked factors.

Dependents, Property Area, and (where included) Loan Amount Term formed a second-ranking group (light gray), followed by Married, Education, Gender, and Self-Employed (dark gray) as minor players.

Test scores and factor rankings vary somewhat from run to run, so relative rankings may shift slightly. In general, however, taking out the Loan Amount and more minor factors decreases accuracy. Log transformations of Total Income and Loan Amount had little effect.
